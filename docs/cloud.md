## What is Nimbus Cloud?

Nimbus Cloud is the **fully managed** version of Nimbus. Instead of running the Docker stack on your own machine or server, the Yoodule team hosts and operates it for you — provisioning your environment, keeping it patched, and monitoring it 24/7 so you don't have to.

You get the **same Nimbus you already know** — the unified MCP gateway, the virtual workspace, the bundled servers — without the operational overhead of running it yourself.

**Self-hosting Nimbus is and always will be free.** Cloud is for teams who'd rather pay us to run it than to run it themselves.

---

## What we handle for you

- **Setup & provisioning** — we configure your environment, your MCP servers, and your integrations end-to-end.
- **Runtime & operations** — the stack stays up, scaled, and patched. You focus on using it, not maintaining it.
- **Monitoring & incident response** — 24/7 health monitoring, alerting, and a real human on call when something goes wrong.
- **Upgrades & migrations** — new Nimbus versions, MCP server updates, and dependency bumps land on your instance without you having to chase them.

---

## Who it's for

- **Teams that don't want to run infrastructure** — you want the gateway, not a part-time SRE job.
- **Production workloads** where uptime, observability, and a real on-call matter more than the cost of a managed plan.
- **Anyone who self-hosts today but wants to offload the operational burden** — we handle the migration, you keep the configuration.
