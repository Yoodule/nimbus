## FAQ

**Can I migrate from self-host to cloud later?**
Yes. One-click import of your `mcp.json`, `.env`, and OAuth tokens. We do the cutover with you on a call.

**Can I migrate from cloud back to self-host?**
Yes. Same one-click export, runs against any Nimbus install.

**Where is my data stored?**
US or EU, your choice at signup. We don't move it without telling you.

**What do you have access to?**
Your Nimbus instance's runtime logs and the MCP server outputs your workflows produce. We don't read your source code, your CRM, or your email contents.

**Can I cancel?**
Yes, any time. You keep your data export.

**What regions are available?**
US-East, US-West, EU-Frankfurt. More on request.
