Nimbus Cloud

Managed Nimbus — we run it, you ship the work.

What you get

  • Ship in days, not months. We provision your Nimbus instance in 24 hours so your team is building workflows, not plumbing.
  • Get 10 hours a week back. No patches, no Docker upgrades, no on-call rotation for the stack — your team focuses on the work only they can do.
  • Sleep through incidents. We monitor 24/7 and page you only when something needs a human decision.
  • Upgrade without disruption. New Nimbus versions roll out with zero downtime, and you approve every change.

How it works

  1. Sign up. A 15-minute call to confirm fit and scope.
  2. We provision. A dedicated Nimbus instance in your region, with your MCP servers and OAuth connections in place.
  3. You connect your tools. Same Nimbus, same MCP servers — nothing new to learn on your side.

FAQ

Can I migrate from self-host to cloud later? Yes. One-click import of your mcp.json, .env, and OAuth tokens. We do the cutover with you on a call.

Can I migrate from cloud back to self-host? Yes. Same one-click export, runs against any Nimbus install.

Where is my data stored? US or EU, your choice at signup. We don't move it without telling you.

What do you have access to? Your Nimbus instance's runtime logs and the MCP server outputs your workflows produce. We don't read your source code, your CRM, or your email contents.

Can I cancel? Yes, any time. You keep your data export.

What regions are available? US-East, US-West, EU-Frankfurt. More on request.
